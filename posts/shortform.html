Is long term planning a prerequisite for AGI? 12/6/23

we're pretty bad at prioritizing long term planning. like one of the biggest struggles with combating climate change is that we struggle to take action on thing that don't immediately affect us. also a similar story when we try to empathize with massive death tolls from conflicts.

i think, as humans we're pretty flawed but we still manage to pursue some vague form of meaningful long term planning and advance. i think that if we create a model with less than optimal, or even sub human "planning", will will reach human levels. combined with the inherent ability to condense thousands of years of human evolution, i feel like a suboptimial architecture of agi would still get us to superhuman levels by iterating on top of itself.
