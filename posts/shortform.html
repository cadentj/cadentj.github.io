Motivation and Ego 12/2/23

I believe motivation is deeply tied to self image.

I recently met several talented individuals and was inspired by their drive and achievement.

All of them excelled in one area as a kid - chess, rowing, math. Their natural talent translated into confidence. Confidence became ego, and ego became motivation.
